<p><p>The vacuum relay market includes various types, primarily categorized as DPDT (Double Pole Double Throw), SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw), and SPST (Single Pole Single Throw). DPDT relays control multiple circuits with two positions, enhancing versatility. SPDT relays manage one circuit with two possible outputs, suitable for switching applications. SPST relays act as simple on-off switches for a single circuit. These types are widely used in automation, telecommunications, and industrial applications due to their reliability and efficiency in electrical control systems.</p></p>
