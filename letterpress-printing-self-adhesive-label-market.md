Release liner labels feature a backing material that supports easy handling and application, often used for products requiring protection during storage or shipping. In contrast, liner-less labels eliminate the backing, allowing for more efficient use of materials and reduced waste, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Both types cater to various industries, including food, cosmetics, and logistics, enhancing branding and product information display.</p></p>
