<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Peripheral Bone Densitometry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (SEXA)</li><li>Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (pDEXA)</li><li>Radiographic Absorptiometry (RA)</li><li>Quantitative Ultrasound (QUS)</li><li>Peripheral Quantitative Computed Tomography (pQCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peripheral Bone Densitometry market comprises various technologies for measuring bone density. Single Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (SEXA) uses a single x-ray energy to assess bone mass. Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (pDEXA) employs two x-ray energies for more precise measurements. Radiographic Absorptiometry (RA) utilizes standard X-rays to estimate bone density. Quantitative Ultrasound (QUS) assesses bone quality through sound waves, while Peripheral Quantitative Computed Tomography (pQCT) offers detailed 3D images of bone structure and density, enhancing diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
