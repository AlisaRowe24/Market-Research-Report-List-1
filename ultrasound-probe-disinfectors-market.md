<p><strong>The Ultrasound Probe Disinfectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intermediate/Low-Level Disinfection (LLDs/ILDs)</li><li>High-Level Disinfection (HLD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound probe disinfection market is segmented into two primary types: Intermediate/Low-Level Disinfection (LLDs/ILDs) and High-Level Disinfection (HLD). LLDs/ILDs are designed for cleaning probes that come into contact with non-intact skin or mucous membranes, offering basic microbial reduction. In contrast, HLD products are used for probes that come into contact with sterile areas or invasive procedures, ensuring a higher level of microbial kill. This distinction is crucial for maintaining infection control standards in healthcare settings.</p></p>
