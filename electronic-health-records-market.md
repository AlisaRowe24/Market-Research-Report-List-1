<p><p>The Electronic Health Records (EHR) market encompasses three main types: web-based, client-server based, and software as a service (SaaS). </p><p>Web-based EHRs allow access via the internet, offering flexibility and remote functionality. Client-server EHRs are installed locally on a facility's servers, providing control and customization but requiring significant IT resources. SaaS EHRs operate on a subscription model, hosted in the cloud, enabling easy updates and scalability while reducing the need for in-house IT infrastructure. Each type caters to different organizational needs and resources.</p></p>
