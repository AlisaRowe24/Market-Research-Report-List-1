<p><strong>The Flat Panel Display (FPD) Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Array Test</li><li>Cell Test</li><li>Module Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flat Panel Display (FPD) inspection equipment market encompasses three main types: Array Test, Cell Test, and Module Test. Array Test focuses on assessing the performance and quality of the pixel array on the glass substrate, ensuring uniformity and defect detection. Cell Test evaluates the individual cells' functionality and efficiency, crucial for OLED and LCD displays. Module Test examines the complete assembly of FPD modules, verifying the interaction between components and overall display performance, critical for ensuring high-quality end products.</p></p>
