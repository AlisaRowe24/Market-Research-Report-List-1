<p><p>The flow control equipment market consists of three main types: automatic control valves, semi-automatic control valves, and manual control valves. Automatic control valves function autonomously to regulate flow based on preset parameters, improving efficiency and precision. Semi-automatic control valves require some manual intervention for operation but can still perform automated adjustments. Manual control valves depend entirely on human operation, offering simplicity and reliability in low-demand scenarios. Each type serves distinct applications across industries such as water treatment, oil and gas, and manufacturing.</p></p>
