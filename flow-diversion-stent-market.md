<p><p>The Flow Diversion Stent market comprises primarily two types: Single Channel and Dual Channel stents. Single Channel stents feature a single pathway for blood flow, allowing for targeted arterial repair and flow modification. In contrast, Dual Channel stents have two independent channels that facilitate both blood flow and enhanced vessel support, providing comprehensive management of complex vascular conditions. These options cater to different patient needs, influencing treatment outcomes and procedural efficiency in neurovascular interventions, such as aneurysm management.</p></p>
