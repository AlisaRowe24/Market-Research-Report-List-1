<p><p>The Weak Acid Cation Exchange Resin (WAC) market is categorized into two main types: Gel Weak Acid Cation Exchange Resins and Macroporous Weak Acid Cation Exchange Resins. Gel WAC resins have a uniform structure, ideal for applications requiring high efficiency in deionization and softening processes. In contrast, Macroporous WAC resins possess a larger pore structure that enhances mass transfer and capacity, making them suitable for more complex applications, including industrial wastewater treatment and selective ion removal processes.</p></p>
