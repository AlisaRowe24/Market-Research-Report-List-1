<p><p>Chip resistors, compact electronic components, serve various applications in the market, including pressure-sensitive and thermosensitive types. Pressure-sensitive chip resistors respond to mechanical forces, adapting their resistance based on applied pressure, making them ideal for sensors and tactile applications. Conversely, thermosensitive chip resistors change resistance with temperature variations, suitable for temperature sensing and thermal management in electronic devices. Both types enhance circuit performance, offering precision and reliability in automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial applications, driving demand in their respective markets.</p></p>
