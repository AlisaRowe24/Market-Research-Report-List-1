<p><p>Single Inlet Centrifugal Brushless DC Fans are categorized by blade design: Forward-curved fans have blades curved in the direction of rotation, offering high airflow at low pressure, ideal for HVAC systems. Backward-curved fans feature blades curved against rotation, providing high efficiency and pressure, suitable for industrial applications. Straight Radial fans have flat, straight blades, delivering medium airflow and pressure, often used in compact or high-static environments. Each type caters to specific performance needs, balancing airflow, pressure, and efficiency.</p></p>
