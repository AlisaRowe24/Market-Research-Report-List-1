<p><p>The pipe fusion machines market is categorized primarily into plastic welding and metal welding. Plastic welding involves using heat and pressure to join thermoplastic pipes, ideal for applications in water supply, gas distribution, and drainage systems. Techniques such as butt fusion and socket fusion are commonly employed. In contrast, metal welding focuses on joining metal pipes, utilizing processes like TIG and MIG welding, essential in industries such as oil and gas, construction, and manufacturing, where durability and strength are critical.</p></p>
