<p><p>The smart meter market is divided into two main types: single-phase smart meters and three-phase smart meters. Single-phase smart meters are primarily used in residential settings, measuring electrical consumption for homes and small businesses. They are simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, three-phase smart meters cater to industrial and commercial users, measuring higher power loads with greater accuracy. This type accommodates complex electrical systems, providing detailed consumption data essential for demand management and energy efficiency initiatives.</p></p>
