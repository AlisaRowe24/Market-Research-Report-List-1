<p><p>The Indium Tin Oxide (ITO) market is primarily categorized into two types: primary-source ITO and reclaim-source ITO. Primary-source ITO is derived from mining processes, utilizing virgin materials that ensure high purity and performance for applications in displays and touchscreens. In contrast, reclaim-source ITO involves recycling existing ITO materials from discarded electronics, offering a sustainable and cost-effective alternative. This reclaimed ITO maintains adequate quality while reducing environmental impact, making both sources critical for meeting the increasing demand in various industries.</p></p>
