<p><strong>The Diaphragm Coupling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Diaphragm</li><li>Double Diaphragm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm coupling market is segmented into single diaphragm and double diaphragm types. Single diaphragm couplings consist of one flexible diaphragm that accommodates misalignment and transmits torque, making them suitable for compact spaces and moderate misalignment. In contrast, double diaphragm couplings feature two diaphragms, enhancing flexibility and load capacity, which is ideal for applications with significant misalignment or higher torque requirements. Both types ensure minimal backlash, efficient power transmission, and durability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
