<p><p>The circuit protection device market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Overcurrent Protection** devices safeguard against excessive current by interrupting the circuit, preventing damage to components. </p><p>2. **Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Protection** devices protect sensitive electronics from sudden voltage spikes caused by electrostatic discharges, ensuring reliable operation.</p><p>3. **Overvoltage Protection** devices, such as surge protectors, prevent voltage spikes from damaging equipment, maintaining the integrity of electrical systems. Together, these devices enhance the reliability and safety of diverse electronic applications.</p></p>
