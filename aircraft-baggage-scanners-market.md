<p><p>The Aircraft Baggage Scanners market includes two primary types: X-ray and Millimeter Wave scanners. X-ray scanners use electromagnetic radiation to create detailed images of baggage contents, detecting prohibited items like weapons or explosives. They are widely used due to their efficiency and accuracy. Millimeter Wave scanners, on the other hand, utilize non-ionizing radio waves to detect concealed objects on passengers or in baggage, offering enhanced security with minimal health risks. Both technologies are integral to aviation security, ensuring safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
