<p><p>The asthma and COPD market is categorized into three main types: anti-inflammatory drugs, bronchodilator monotherapy, and combination drugs. Anti-inflammatory drugs, such as corticosteroids, reduce airway inflammation, providing long-term control. Bronchodilator monotherapy focuses on quick relief by relaxing smooth muscle in the airways, improving airflow. Combination drugs incorporate both anti-inflammatory and bronchodilator properties, offering comprehensive management of symptoms for patients. Each category addresses varying severity and individual needs in asthma and COPD treatment, enhancing overall patient care.</p></p>
