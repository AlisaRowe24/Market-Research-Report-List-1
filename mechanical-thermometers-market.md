<p><p>The mechanical thermometers market includes three primary types: Bimetal thermometers utilize two metal strips that expand at different rates to indicate temperature. Gas-actuated thermometers use the expansion of gas in a sealed chamber to provide precise temperature readings. Liquid expansion thermometers rely on the thermal expansion of liquids, typically mercury or alcohol, to indicate temperature changes on a calibrated scale. Each type offers unique advantages and applications, catering to different industries such as HVAC, food processing, and manufacturing.</p></p>
