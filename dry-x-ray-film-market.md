<p><ul><li>Laser Imaging Film</li><li>Thermal Sensitive Film</li><li>Inkjet Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry X-ray film market consists of three primary types: laser imaging film, thermal sensitive film, and inkjet film. Laser imaging film is designed for use with laser printers, offering high-resolution images and quick processing. Thermal sensitive film responds to heat, allowing images to be developed without liquid chemicals, enhancing convenience. Inkjet film is compatible with inkjet printers, providing versatility for various imaging needs. Each type serves distinct applications in medical imaging, industrial inspection, and documentation processes.</p></p>
