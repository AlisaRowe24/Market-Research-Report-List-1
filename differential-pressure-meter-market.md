<p><ul><li>Piston Style</li><li>Diaphragm Type</li><li>Bellows differential Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The differential pressure meter market includes various types, primarily piston style, diaphragm type, and bellows differential type. Piston style meters use a movable piston to measure pressure differences, offering high accuracy and stability. Diaphragm type meters employ a flexible diaphragm that responds to pressure changes, suitable for various applications due to their reliability. Bellows differential meters utilize a set of bellows to measure pressure differences, providing good sensitivity and versatility in handling corrosive fluids. Each type serves specific needs across industries.</p></p>
