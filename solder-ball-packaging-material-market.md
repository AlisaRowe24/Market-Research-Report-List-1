<p><p>The Solder Ball Packaging Material market is categorized into two main types: Lead Solder Balls and Lead-Free Solder Balls. Lead Solder Balls contain lead as a primary alloying element and are traditionally used in electronic components to ensure reliable connections. However, due to health and environmental concerns, Lead-Free Solder Balls have gained popularity, utilizing metals like tin, silver, and copper to meet strict regulatory standards. The shift toward lead-free options reflects a growing emphasis on sustainability and safety in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
