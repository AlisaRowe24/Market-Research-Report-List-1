<p><strong>The Cheese Coagulation Detection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cheese coagulation detection systems play a crucial role in the cheese-making process, helping to determine the optimal time for curd formation. The market for these systems is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact. Contact systems involve physical sensors that directly interact with the milk or curd, providing real-time measurements of coagulation properties. In contrast, non-contact systems utilize advanced technologies like ultrasound or infrared sensors to assess coagulation without direct interaction, offering benefits such as reduced contamination risk and easier maintenance.</p></p>
