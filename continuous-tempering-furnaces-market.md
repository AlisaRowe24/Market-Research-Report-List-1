<p><p>The continuous tempering furnaces market features distinct types, including pusher type and conveyor type furnaces. Pusher type furnaces utilize a series of pushing mechanisms to move materials steadily through heating zones, ideal for large, uniform batches. Conveyor type furnaces employ a continuous belt system to transport products for uniform heating, suitable for diverse material shapes. Other types may include batch-mode or hybrid systems, catering to specific industrial applications, enhancing process efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
