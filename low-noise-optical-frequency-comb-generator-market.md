<p><p>The Low-Noise Optical Frequency Comb Generator market features two primary types: femtosecond generators and microcavity generators. Femtosecond generators produce extremely short pulses of light, allowing precise measurements and applications in spectroscopy and metrology. They are known for their broad linewidth and stability. Microcavity generators, on the other hand, utilize small optical resonators to create frequency combs with reduced noise levels, making them suitable for high-precision measurements in telecommunications and sensing applications. Together, they enhance the performance of optical systems.</p></p>
