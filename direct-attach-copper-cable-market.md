<p><p>The Direct Attach Copper (DAC) cable market consists of several types, including SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable), SFP+ (enhanced SFP), QSFP/QSFP+ (Quad Small Form-factor Pluggable), XFP (10 Gigabit Form-factor Pluggable), and CXP (100 Gigabit CXP). SFP and SFP+ are widely used for short-distance connections, while QSFP/QSFP+ can support multiple channels for higher data rates. XFP is designed for 10 Gbps connections, and CXP supports even faster speeds, catering to various network demands. Other types may include custom or emerging technologies.</p></p>
