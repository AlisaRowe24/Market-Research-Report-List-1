<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) charging station market includes two primary types: DC Charging Piles and AC Charging Piles. DC Charging Piles provide rapid charging by converting AC power to DC directly, ideal for quick top-ups in public or commercial settings. AC Charging Piles deliver slower charging, requiring the vehicle's onboard charger to convert AC to DC, commonly used for residential or overnight charging. Both cater to diverse charging needs, balancing speed, infrastructure, and cost for NEV users.</p></p>
