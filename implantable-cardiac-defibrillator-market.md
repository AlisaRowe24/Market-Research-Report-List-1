<p><p>The implantable cardiac defibrillator (ICD) market is categorized into single chamber and dual chamber devices. Single chamber ICDs monitor and treat arrhythmias from one heart chamber, typically the right ventricle, making them suitable for patients with specific rhythm issues. In contrast, dual chamber ICDs connect to both the right atrium and ventricle, providing more comprehensive monitoring and pacing capabilities. This flexibility allows for better management of complex arrhythmias, enhancing patient outcomes and expanding market appeal.</p></p>
