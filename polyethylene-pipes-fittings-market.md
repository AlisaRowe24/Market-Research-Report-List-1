<p><p>The polyethylene pipes and fittings market is categorized into several types based on density and structure. Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) is flexible and resistant to impact and chemicals, making it suitable for low-pressure applications. High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) offers enhanced strength and durability, ideal for water supply and gas distribution. Cross-Linked Polyethylene (PEX or XLPE) provides improved temperature resistance and flexibility, commonly used in plumbing and heating systems. Additionally, "Others" encompass specialty grades and composites tailored for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
