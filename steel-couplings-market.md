<p><ul><li>Rigid Coupling</li><li>Flexible Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel couplings market is primarily categorized into rigid and flexible couplings. Rigid couplings provide a solid connection between shafts, ensuring accurate alignment and torque transfer, making them ideal for high-precision applications. In contrast, flexible couplings accommodate misalignment and shock loads, offering greater versatility in connecting rotating elements. These couplings are used across various industries, including manufacturing and construction, to enhance efficiency and reliability in mechanical systems. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing performance and minimizing wear.</p></p>
