<p><strong>The Hydraulic Elevator System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Holed Hydraulic</li><li>Holeless Hydraulic</li><li>Roped Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic elevator system market comprises three main types: holed hydraulic, holeless hydraulic, and roped hydraulic. Holed hydraulic elevators utilize a cylinder deep within a pit to move the cab up and down; they are well-suited for low-rise buildings. Holeless hydraulic elevators, without a deep pit, employ telescopic cylinders, ideal for mid-rise applications. Roped hydraulic systems combine hydraulic power with ropes for better efficiency and speed, often used in taller buildings where space constraints exist.</p></p>
