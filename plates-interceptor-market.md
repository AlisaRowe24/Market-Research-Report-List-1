<p><ul><li>CPI (Corrugated Plate Interceptor)</li><li>TPI (Tilted Plate Interceptor)</li><li>PPI (Parallel Plate Interceptor)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plates Interceptor market encompasses three primary types: Corrugated Plate Interceptors (CPI), Tilted Plate Interceptors (TPI), and Parallel Plate Interceptors (PPI). CPI utilizes corrugated plates to optimize sedimentation, enhancing water treatment efficiency. TPI employs tilted plates to facilitate better solid separation, making it suitable for various applications. PPI, characterized by parallel plates, maximizes surface area for sedimentation, effectively removing particulates. These interceptors are vital in wastewater management, offering efficient solutions for enhancing water quality across multiple industries.</p></p>
