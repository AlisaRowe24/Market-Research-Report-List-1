<p><p>Diaphragm couplings are flexible mechanical components that connect shafts while accommodating misalignment and reducing vibration. The market is primarily segmented into single diaphragm and double diaphragm couplings. Single diaphragm couplings feature one flexible diaphragm, suitable for applications with limited space and moderate misalignment. In contrast, double diaphragm couplings employ two diaphragms, providing enhanced torque capacity and better alignment compensation, making them ideal for high-power applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
