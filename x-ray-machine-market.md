<p><p>The X-ray machine market is primarily segmented into analog and digital X-ray machines. Analog X-ray machines use traditional film-based technology to capture images, offering a straightforward, cost-effective solution but requiring more processing time and physical storage. In contrast, digital X-ray machines utilize advanced sensors to produce high-quality images instantly, allowing for easier data sharing and enhanced image analysis. This transition towards digital technology is driven by efficiency, improved diagnostics, and reduced radiation exposure, reshaping the healthcare landscape.</p></p>
