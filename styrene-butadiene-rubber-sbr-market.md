<p><p>Styrene-butadiene rubber (SBR) is a synthetic rubber widely used in various applications, particularly in tires and automotive components. The market is primarily categorized into two types: emulsion SBR (ESBR) and solution SBR (SSBR). Emulsion SBR is produced through an emulsion polymerization process, offering good abrasion resistance and low-temperature flexibility, making it suitable for tire treads. Solution SBR, on the other hand, provides enhanced strength and processing capabilities, ideal for high-performance applications, including high-traffic tires and specialty rubber products.</p></p>
