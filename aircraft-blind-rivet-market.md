<p><strong>The Aircraft Blind Rivet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>With Bucking Bars</li><li>Without Bucking bars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft blind rivet market can be categorized into two main types: those that utilize bucking bars and those that do not. Rivets with bucking bars involve a secondary tool to secure and compress the rivet while it is being installed, ensuring a strong bond and reducing the risk of deformation. In contrast, rivets without bucking bars rely solely on the rivet gun for installation, making them simpler and faster to use in certain applications, often preferred for efficiency and ease of handling.</p></p>
