<p><strong>The Flexo Printing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Impression (CI) Flexo Presses</li><li>Stack Flexo Presses</li><li>In-line Flexo Presses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexo printing machine market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Central Impression (CI) Flexo Presses** feature a central cylinder where multiple anilox rolls transfer ink to the substrate, ideal for high-quality prints on flexible materials.</p><p>2. **Stack Flexo Presses** have printing units stacked vertically, allowing easy setup for short runs and diverse substrates, making them versatile for various applications.</p><p>3. **In-line Flexo Presses** integrate with other processes, offering seamless production for labels and packaging, maximizing efficiency in single-pass operations.</p></p>
