<p><p>The passive damper market comprises hydraulic and pneumatic types, each serving distinct applications in vibration and shock absorption. Hydraulic dampers utilize fluid to dissipate energy, offering high reliability and efficiency for heavy machinery and automotive systems. In contrast, pneumatic dampers use air or gas to achieve damping, providing lightweight solutions ideal for applications requiring flexibility and quick response, such as in aerospace and consumer products. Both types are crucial in enhancing the stability and longevity of various structures and vehicles.</p></p>
