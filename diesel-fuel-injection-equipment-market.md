<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System</li><li>Common Rail Injection System</li><li>Other Injection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diesel Fuel Injection Equipment market encompasses various injection systems, each with distinct mechanisms. The Pump-Line-Nozzle (PLN) system uses a high-pressure pump to send fuel directly to the nozzle, allowing precise timing and atomization. The Common Rail Injection System stores fuel at high pressure in a common rail, facilitating multiple injections per cycle for improved efficiency and emissions. Other injection systems may include unit injector systems or electronically controlled methods, each aiming to optimize performance and fuel economy in diesel engines.</p></p>
