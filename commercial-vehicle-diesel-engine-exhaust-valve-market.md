<p><p>The commercial vehicle diesel engine exhaust valve market is primarily categorized into three types: mono metallic valves, bimetallic valves, and hollow valves. Mono metallic valves are made from a single type of metal, providing durability and heat resistance. Bimetallic valves combine two different metals to enhance performance, offering improved thermal stability and resistance to wear. Hollow valves feature a lightweight design with reduced material use, aiding fuel efficiency while maintaining strength. Each type serves specific performance demands in commercial diesel engines.</p></p>
