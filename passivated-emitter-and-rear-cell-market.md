<p><p>The Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell (PERC) is a type of solar cell technology that enhances the efficiency of photovoltaic systems by incorporating a passivation layer on the rear side of the cell. PERC cells are increasingly favored for their ability to improve performance in lower-light conditions, contributing to their rising adoption in the solar energy market.</p><p>The PERC market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the global shift towards renewable energy sources and advancements in solar technology.
