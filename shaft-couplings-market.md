<p><p>The shaft couplings market is primarily categorized into flexible and rigid couplings. Flexible couplings accommodate misalignment between shafts, allowing for some movement, which helps reduce wear and stress, making them ideal for dynamic applications. Rigid couplings, on the other hand, provide a solid connection between shafts, ensuring precise alignment and torque transfer, suitable for static applications. Both types play crucial roles in machinery by enhancing performance, reducing vibration, and improving overall efficiency in various industrial settings.</p></p>
