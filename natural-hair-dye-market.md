<p><strong>The Natural Hair Dye Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Temporary Hair Dye</li><li>Semi-& Demi-Permanent Hair Dye</li><li>Permanent Hair Dye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural hair dye market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Temporary Hair Dye**: These dyes provide a short-lived color, washing out easily after a few shampoos. They are ideal for experimentation or special occasions.</p><p>2. **Semi-Permanent Hair Dye**: This type lasts longer, typically up to 8-12 washes, offering a vibrant hue without the commitment of permanent options.</p><p>3. **Demi-Permanent Hair Dye**: These dyes penetrate the hair shaft more deeply than semi-permanent but do not provide full gray coverage, lasting around 12-24 shampoos.</p><p>4. **Permanent Hair Dye**: Permanents offer lasting color that can cover grays effectively, as they diffuse into the hair structure, maintaining color until new growth occurs.</p></p>
