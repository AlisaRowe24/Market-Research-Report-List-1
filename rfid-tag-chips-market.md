<p><strong>The RFID Tag Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HF RFID Chip</li><li>UHF RFID Chip</li><li>LF RFID Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID tag chip market is categorized into three primary types: HF (High Frequency), UHF (Ultra High Frequency), and LF (Low Frequency) RFID chips. HF RFID chips operate typically at 13.56 MHz, suitable for asset tracking and contactless payments. UHF RFID chips, functioning between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, enable long-range communication, ideal for supply chain management and inventory control. LF RFID chips, usually around 125 kHz to 134 kHz, are commonly used for animal tagging and access control, offering short-range read capabilities.</p></p>
