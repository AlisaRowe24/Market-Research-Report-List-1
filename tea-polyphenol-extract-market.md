The ion precipitation method involves altering ionic conditions to precipitate polyphenols from tea extracts. Low-temperature purification employs chill techniques to maintain compound integrity during extraction. Additionally, enzymatic methods use specific enzymes to enhance polyphenol recovery by breaking down cell walls. Each method offers unique benefits in efficiency, purity, and environmental impact, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
