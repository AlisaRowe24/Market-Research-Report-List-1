<p><strong>The Magnesium Alloy Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast</li><li>Forged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnesium alloy wheels are lightweight, durable, and enhance vehicle performance. The market is divided into **cast** and **forged** types. **Cast magnesium wheels** are made by pouring molten alloy into molds, offering cost-effectiveness and versatility in design but with slightly lower strength. **Forged magnesium wheels** are created by compressing alloy under high pressure, resulting in superior strength, lighter weight, and higher performance, but at a premium cost. Both types cater to automotive and motorsport industries, balancing performance, weight reduction, and cost.</p></p>
