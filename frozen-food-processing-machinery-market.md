<p><p>The frozen food processing machinery market encompasses various methods, primarily freezing and drying. Freezing technology involves rapidly lowering temperatures to preserve food quality, texture, and nutrients, ensuring long shelf life. Common freezing equipment includes blast freezers and cryogenic freezers. In contrast, drying methods, such as freeze-drying and dehydrating, remove moisture from foods, extending shelf life while maintaining flavor and nutritional content. Both methods cater to the increasing demand for convenient, ready-to-eat meals in the global food market.</p></p>
