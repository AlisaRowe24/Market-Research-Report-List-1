<p><strong>The Dental Ultrasonic Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental ultrasonic device market is primarily segmented into two types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric devices. Magnetostrictive devices utilize electromagnetic energy to generate ultrasonic vibrations in the metal stack, effectively removing dental plaque and calculus. In contrast, piezoelectric devices rely on ceramic crystals that change shape under electrical stimulation, producing precise ultrasonic vibrations. Both types enhance dental hygiene through efficient scaling and cleaning, but they differ in design, performance, and efficiency, catering to varying dental practice needs and preferences.</p></p>
