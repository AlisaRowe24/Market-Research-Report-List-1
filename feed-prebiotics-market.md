<p><p>The Feed Prebiotics Market includes types such as Inulin, Fructooligosaccharides (FOS), and Isomaltooligosaccharides (IMOs). Inulin is a soluble fiber that promotes gut health and enhances nutrient absorption. FOS, derived from fruits and vegetables, stimulates beneficial gut bacteria and improves digestion. IMOs, sourced from starch, support healthy gut microbiota and boost immunity. These prebiotics are increasingly utilized in animal feed to improve overall health, growth performance, and feed efficiency in livestock and poultry production.</p></p>
