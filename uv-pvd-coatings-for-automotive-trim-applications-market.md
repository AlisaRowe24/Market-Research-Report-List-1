<p><p>UV PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coatings for automotive trim applications consist of three primary market types: UV Base-Coat, UV Mid-Coat, and UV Top-Coat. The UV Base-Coat provides an initial protective layer, enhancing adhesion and ensuring color stability. The UV Mid-Coat offers additional durability and can add subtle textures or effects. Finally, the UV Top-Coat serves as the outermost layer, delivering enhanced scratch resistance, gloss, and UV stability, ultimately ensuring long-lasting aesthetics and protection for automotive trims.</p></p>
