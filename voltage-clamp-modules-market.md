<p><p>The Voltage Clamp Modules market is categorized into Single Electrode Voltage Clamp and Two Electrode Voltage Clamp types. Single Electrode Voltage Clamp allows for the measurement of ion currents through a single patch of membrane, offering precise control of the membrane potential. In contrast, the Two Electrode Voltage Clamp enables the simultaneous measurement and control of two separate electrodes, often used in larger cells, providing enhanced spatial resolution and better characterization of cellular ionic currents. Both types are crucial for electrophysiological research.</p></p>
