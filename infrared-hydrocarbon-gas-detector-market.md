<p><p>The infrared hydrocarbon gas detector market includes two primary types: point infrared (IR) detectors and open path IR detectors. Point IR detectors are used to monitor specific, localized areas for gas leaks, providing rapid detection and high sensitivity. In contrast, open path IR detectors cover larger areas by sending a beam of infrared light across a defined path, allowing for the continuous monitoring of gas concentrations over extended distances. Both types enhance safety and are essential in various industrial applications.</p></p>
