<p><p>The tennis vibrator market comprises three main types: granular, ribbon, and cuboid. Granular vibrators use small particles to create vibrations, suitable for applications requiring fine control. Ribbon vibrators feature a longer, flexible design that allows for uniform distribution of energy across a surface, ideal for processing larger materials. Cuboid vibrators, with their compact, box-like shape, are designed for strong, directed vibrations, making them effective for breaking apart compacted materials. Each type caters to specific vibration needs in various industries.</p></p>
