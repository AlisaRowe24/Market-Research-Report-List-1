<p><p>Fog Computing is a decentralized computing infrastructure that brings computing resources closer to the data source, particularly in IoT environments. This architecture enhances the performance of applications by processing data at the edge of the network instead of relying solely on centralized cloud services. With the proliferation of IoT devices and the increasing demand for real-time data processing, fog computing facilitates reduced latency, improved bandwidth efficiency, and enhanced security.</p><p>The Fog Computing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.6% during the forecast period, driven by the need for efficient data management solutions and the rise of smart city initiatives. Additionally, advancements in networking technologies, such as 5G, are further propelling the growth of fog computing by enabling faster data transfer and connectivity.
