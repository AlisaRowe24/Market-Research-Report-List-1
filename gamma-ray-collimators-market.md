<p><ul><li>Panoramic Collimators</li><li>Directional Collimators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gamma ray collimators are devices that selectively filter gamma radiation to enhance imaging quality in medical and industrial applications. The panoramic collimator allows for a wide field of view, capturing more extensive areas at once, which is useful in whole-body scans. In contrast, directional collimators focus on a specific angle, enhancing resolution and sensitivity in targeted imaging. Both types optimize the detection of gamma rays while reducing background noise, making them critical in diagnostics and research settings.</p></p>
