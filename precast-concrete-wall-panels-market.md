<p><ul><li>Load-Bearing Wall</li><li>Non-load Bearing Wall</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precast concrete wall panels are prefabricated units used in construction, categorized into load-bearing and non-load-bearing types. Load-bearing wall panels support structural loads, such as the weight of floors and roofs, ensuring stability and strength. Non-load-bearing wall panels, also known as partition walls, do not carry structural loads and are primarily used for space division, insulation, or aesthetic purposes. Both types offer durability, efficiency, and design flexibility, catering to diverse construction needs while reducing on-site labor and construction time.</p></p>
