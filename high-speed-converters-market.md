<p><ul><li>High Speed ADC Converters</li><li>High Speed DAC Converters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed converters market comprises two primary types: high-speed ADC (Analog-to-Digital Converters) and high-speed DAC (Digital-to-Analog Converters). High-speed ADCs convert analog signals into digital form with fast sampling rates, ensuring precise data capture for applications like telecommunications and instrumentation. Conversely, high-speed DACs transform digital signals back into analog, facilitating real-time signal processing in areas such as audio and video broadcasting. Both types are crucial for modern systems requiring rapid data conversion and high performance across various industries.</p></p>
