<p><p>Powder-free disposable medical gloves are essential in healthcare settings, offering protection and hygiene during medical procedures. The main types include PVC, nitrile, and vinyl gloves. PVC gloves are economical and suitable for low-risk tasks but lack elasticity. Nitrile gloves are more durable, providing superior puncture resistance and a better fit, making them ideal for high-risk applications. Vinyl gloves offer a latex-free alternative that is comfortable for short-term use. Each type serves different needs based on protection level and comfort.</p></p>
