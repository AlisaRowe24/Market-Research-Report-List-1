<p><p>The Pro Headphone market comprises three main types: over-ear, in-ear, and on-ear. Over-ear headphones feature larger ear cups that fully encompass the ears, providing superior sound isolation and comfort, making them ideal for studio environments. In-ear headphones fit snugly within the ear canal, offering portability and excellent noise isolation, suitable for musicians on the go. On-ear headphones rest on the outer ear, balancing comfort and compact design, often favored for casual listening while maintaining decent sound quality.</p></p>
