<p><p>Track Circuit Reader (TCR) antennas are used in railway signaling systems to detect train positions through track circuits. The market for TCR antennas is primarily divided into two types: DC Track Circuit Reader Antennas and AC Track Circuit Reader Antennas. DC TCR antennas support direct current-based track circuits, optimizing reliability and performance in specific conditions. In contrast, AC TCR antennas are designed for alternating current track circuits, catering to a broader range of signaling applications and enhancing system versatility.</p></p>
