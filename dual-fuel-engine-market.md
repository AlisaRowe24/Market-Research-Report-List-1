<p><strong>The Dual Fuel Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Four-Stroke</li><li>Two-Stroke</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dual fuel engine market is segmented into four-stroke and two-stroke types, each offering distinct operational advantages. Four-stroke engines, known for their efficiency and lower emissions, operate on a cycle that completes a power stroke every two revolutions. In contrast, two-stroke engines complete a power cycle in a single revolution, allowing for higher power output but typically at the cost of increased emissions. Both types are leveraged based on application needs in marine, industrial, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
