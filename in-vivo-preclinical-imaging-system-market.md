<p><p>The in vivo preclinical imaging system market comprises various imaging modalities. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) provides detailed soft tissue images, ideal for neurological and cardiovascular studies. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) allows for metabolic and physiological assessments through radiotracers. Micro Computed Tomography (micro-CT) offers high-resolution 3D imaging, particularly useful for skeletal and tissue analysis. Optical Imaging leverages fluorescent and bioluminescent signals for real-time monitoring of biological processes. Other methods may include ultrasound and photoacoustic imaging, catering to diverse research needs.</p></p>
