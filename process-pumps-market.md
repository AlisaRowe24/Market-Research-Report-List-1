<p><p>The process pumps market encompasses various types suited for different applications. Axial flow pumps move fluid along the pump axis, ideal for high flow, low-pressure scenarios. Centrifugal pumps use rotational energy to direct fluid in a circular motion, prevalent in a wide range of industries for their efficiency. Multistage pumps consist of multiple impellers to achieve high pressure and flow rates, suited for applications requiring significant elevation. Other market types include diaphragm and peristaltic pumps, catering to specialized needs.</p></p>
