<p><p>The resin-based paste market is primarily categorized into two types: epoxy resin-based and methacrylate resin-based products. Epoxy resin-based pastes are known for their strong adhesion, chemical resistance, and durability, making them suitable for applications in construction, automotive, and electronics. In contrast, methacrylate resin-based pastes offer rapid curing times and excellent bonding strength, making them ideal for applications in dental, automotive, and jewelry industries. Both types serve specific needs across various sectors, driving their market demand.</p></p>
