<p><p>The Pressure Safety Valve (PSV) market comprises three main types: Spring Operated Valves, Pilot Operated Valves, and Dead Weight Valves. Spring Operated Valves utilize a spring mechanism to open and release pressure when set limits are exceeded, commonly used in various industries. Pilot Operated Valves rely on a pilot system for greater efficiency and sensitivity, suitable for high-pressure applications. Dead Weight Valves function based on gravitational force, offering accuracy in pressure control, often found in laboratory and calibration settings.</p></p>
