<p><ul><li>Infrared Thermal Imaging</li><li>Microwave Thermal imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld thermal imaging equipment typically includes two main types: infrared thermal imaging and microwave thermal imaging. Infrared thermal imaging devices detect heat emitted from objects and convert it into visible images, making them ideal for applications like building inspections and electrical maintenance. Microwave thermal imaging, on the other hand, utilizes higher frequency wavelengths to penetrate various materials, providing detailed insights into subsurface temperatures. Both types offer portable solutions for quick thermal assessments across multiple industries, enhancing safety and efficiency.</p></p>
