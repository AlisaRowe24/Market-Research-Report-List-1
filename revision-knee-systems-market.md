<p><p>The Revision Knee Systems market encompasses three main components: femoral, tibial, and patellar systems. The femoral component involves the replacement of the thighbone part of the knee joint, addressing issues like wear or implant failure. The tibial component pertains to the replacement of the lower leg bone section, ensuring stability and function. Lastly, the patellar component focuses on the replacement of the kneecap, improving mobility and reducing pain. Together, these components facilitate effective knee revisions for patients.</p></p>
