<p><ul><li>PTO-driven</li><li>Walk-behind</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spading machine market is primarily divided into two types: PTO-driven and walk-behind models. PTO-driven spading machines are attached to tractors, utilizing power take-off systems for enhanced efficiency and performance, making them suitable for larger agricultural operations. In contrast, walk-behind spading machines are designed for smaller tasks and gardens, requiring manual operation. They are typically lighter, more compact, and easier to maneuver, catering to homeowners and small-scale farmers. Both types serve distinct preferences and operational needs in soil cultivation.</p></p>
