<p><p>The Golf Rangefinder market comprises two primary types: laser and GPS rangefinders. Laser golf rangefinders utilize infrared laser technology to accurately measure distances to specific targets on the course, providing real-time feedback for players seeking precision. In contrast, GPS golf rangefinders use satellite technology to offer information about course layouts, including distances to hazards and greens. Each type caters to different player preferences, with laser rangefinders favored for exact measurements, while GPS models are popular for ease of use and course navigation.</p></p>
