<p><p>The automotive cooling fan belt market includes three main types: Triangle type, Wedge belt type, and Timing belt type. Triangle type belts are flexible and widely used for their ease of installation and efficiency. Wedge belts offer better grip and are designed to handle higher loads, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Timing belts, known for their precise timing and synchronization, are essential for engines requiring accurate rotational timing. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance and vehicle design requirements.</p></p>
