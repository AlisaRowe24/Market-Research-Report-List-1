<p><strong>The Car Diffuser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Car Diffuser</li><li>Electric Heat Car Diffuser</li><li>Evaporated Car Diffuser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car diffuser market comprises three main types: ultrasonic, electric heat, and evaporated car diffusers. Ultrasonic car diffusers use high-frequency vibrations to disperse essential oils into the air, creating a fine mist. Electric heat diffusers utilize a heating element to warm oils and release their aroma gradually. Evaporated car diffusers rely on passive evaporation, where essential oils are absorbed by a wick or pad, allowing the scent to disperse naturally. Each type offers unique benefits for enhancing the driving experience with pleasant fragrances.</p></p>
