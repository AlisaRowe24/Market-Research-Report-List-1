<p><p>Wafer Level Chip Scale Packaging (WLCSP) is an advanced packaging technology that allows the packaging of semiconductor devices at the wafer level, resulting in smaller footprints and enhanced performance characteristics. WLCSP is increasingly favored in applications requiring high-density interconnections, such as mobile devices, IoT devices, and consumer electronics.</p><p>The WLCSP market is experiencing significant growth, driven by rising demands for miniaturized electronic components and the proliferation of smart devices.
