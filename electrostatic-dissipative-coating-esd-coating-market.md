<p><p>Electrostatic Dissipative (ESD) coatings are specialized finishes designed to manage static electricity, preventing damage to sensitive electronic components. The market for ESD coatings is primarily segmented into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based ESD coatings offer low toxicity, quick drying, and are environmentally friendly, making them popular for indoor applications. In contrast, solvent-based ESD coatings provide superior durability and adhesion, suitable for harsher environments. Both types are essential for enhancing safety and performance in electronics manufacturing and handling.</p></p>
