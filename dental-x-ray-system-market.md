<p><p>The dental X-ray system market is primarily divided into two types: intraoral and extraoral X-ray imaging. Intraoral X-ray imaging captures detailed images of the teeth and surrounding structures from within the mouth, making it essential for diagnosing dental issues. Extraoral X-ray imaging, on the other hand, encompasses broader views by capturing images from outside the mouth, allowing for evaluations of overall oral health, including jaw structure and bone conditions. Both types play crucial roles in comprehensive dental diagnostics.</p></p>
