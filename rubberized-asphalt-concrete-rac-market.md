<p><p>Rubberized Asphalt Concrete (RAC) incorporates recycled rubber into asphalt mixtures, enhancing durability and performance. This market is primarily segmented into two types: Asphalt-rubber, where ground tire rubber is blended with asphalt cement, allowing for flexible, weather-resistant pavements; and Terminal Blend, where rubber is pre-blended with asphalt at a production facility, ensuring consistent quality and improved binder properties. Both types contribute to sustainable practices by recycling waste tires while improving road performance and longevity.</p></p>
