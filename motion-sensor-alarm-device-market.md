<p><p>The motion sensor alarm device market comprises several types, including Passive Infrared (PIR) sensors, Microwave (MW) sensors, and other variations. PIR sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by warm bodies, making them effective for indoor security. Microwave sensors utilize electromagnetic waves to detect motion, offering a longer range and the ability to penetrate walls. Other types may include dual-technology sensors, combining PIR and MW for enhanced accuracy, or advanced technologies like laser and ultrasonic sensors, catering to diverse security needs and applications.</p></p>
