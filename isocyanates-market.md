<p><p>Isocyanates are organic compounds used primarily in the production of polyurethanes. The three main types are Aromatic Diisocyanate (ADI), Methylenediphenyl Diisocyanate (MDI), and Toluene Diisocyanate (TDI). ADI is favored for flexible foam production and coatings, MDI is commonly used in rigid foam and automotive applications due to its superior thermal properties, while TDI is primarily used in flexible foam and elastomers. The market for these isocyanates is driven by demand in construction, automotive, and furniture industries.</p></p>
