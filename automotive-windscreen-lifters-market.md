<p><p>The automotive windscreen lifters market comprises two primary types: electrically-operated and mechanical windscreen lifters. Electrically-operated windscreen lifters utilize electric motors to raise and lower the glass, offering convenience and precision with the push of a button. In contrast, mechanical windscreen lifters rely on manual mechanisms, such as levers or cranks, providing a more traditional and cost-effective solution. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and vehicle designs, impacting overall performance, ease of use, and installation complexity.</p></p>
