<p><ul><li>Manual Winding</li><li>Automatic Winding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical sport watch market can be categorized into two main types: manual winding and automatic winding. Manual winding watches require the wearer to regularly wind the crown to power the movement, offering a traditional experience. In contrast, automatic winding watches harness the kinetic energy generated by the wearer’s wrist movement, allowing the watch to self-wind. Both types appeal to enthusiasts for their craftsmanship, precision, and heritage, catering to different preferences in usability and maintenance.</p></p>
