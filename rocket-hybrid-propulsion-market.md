<p><p>The Rocket Hybrid Propulsion market is divided into two main types: chemical propulsion and non-chemical propulsion. Chemical propulsion utilizes a combination of liquid and solid propellants to generate thrust through combustion, commonly used in traditional rockets. In contrast, non-chemical propulsion encompasses technology such as electric and nuclear propulsion, which relies on electromagnetic or nuclear reactions to produce thrust. Both types contribute to advancements in space exploration, with chemical systems offering high thrust and non-chemical systems providing efficiency and longevity in orbit.</p></p>
