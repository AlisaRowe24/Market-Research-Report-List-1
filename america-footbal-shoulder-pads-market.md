<p><p>The American football shoulder pads market includes two main types: cantilevered and non-cantilevered. Cantilevered pads feature an extended arch over the shoulders, providing enhanced protection for the collarbone and upper chest, ideal for high-impact positions like linemen. Non-cantilevered pads are flatter and lighter, offering greater mobility, preferred by skill positions like quarterbacks and receivers. Both designs prioritize safety and performance, catering to different player needs based on position, impact level, and comfort preferences in the game.</p></p>
