<p><p>The automotive interior plastic components market includes various materials, each serving different applications. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is cost-effective and versatile for interior surfaces. Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) is strong and impact-resistant, used in consoles and clusters. Polycarbonates (PC) are known for transparency and toughness, often used in lighting and displays. Other materials may include specialty plastics for niche applications.</p></p>
