<p><p>The machining tool steel market is categorized into several types, each tailored for specific applications. Hot-work steel is designed for high temperatures, making it ideal for hot forming processes. Cold-work steel, conversely, is used for tools operating at lower temperatures, suitable for cutting and shaping materials. Plastics mold steel is specifically formulated for producing injection molds. High speed steel is renowned for its ability to cut at high speeds, maintaining toughness and wear resistance. Other types may include specialty alloys for niche applications.</p></p>
