<p><ul><li>Polyisobutenyl Succinic Anhydride (PIBSA) Based</li><li>Span 80</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The explosive emulsifier market consists primarily of two types: Polyisobutenyl Succinic Anhydride (PIBSA) based emulsifiers and Span 80. PIBSA serves as a versatile emulsifier, enhancing the stability of explosive formulations by providing excellent adhesion and dispersion characteristics. In contrast, Span 80 is a non-ionic surfactant known for its ability to stabilize emulsions, improving the mixing of oil and water components in explosive compounds. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing the performance and safety of explosive materials.</p></p>
