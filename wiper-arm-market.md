<p><p>The wiper arm market is primarily divided into two types: intermittent and automatic induction wipers. Intermittent wipers operate by allowing the user to control the frequency of the wiping motion, providing flexibility during light rain. In contrast, automatic induction wipers are equipped with sensors that automatically adjust the wipe speed based on rainfall intensity, ensuring optimal visibility without user intervention. Both types enhance driving safety and comfort, catering to different consumer preferences and weather conditions.</p></p>
