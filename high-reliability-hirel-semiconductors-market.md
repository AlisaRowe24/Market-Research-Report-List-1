<p><p>The High Reliability (HiRel) semiconductors market is segmented into Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Through Hole Technology (THT). SMT features components soldered directly onto the surface of circuit boards, offering advantages such as compactness and efficient production processes, while THT involves inserting leads through holes in printed circuit boards, providing robust mechanical connections suitable for high-stress environments. Both technologies cater to industries demanding reliability, such as aerospace and defense, ensuring optimal performance in critical applications.</p></p>
