<p><p>The in-ceiling speaker market is primarily divided into two types: active and passive in-ceiling speakers. Active in-ceiling speakers are self-powered, featuring built-in amplifiers, making them easy to install and ideal for users seeking straightforward setups. In contrast, passive in-ceiling speakers require an external amplifier for power, offering greater flexibility in sound customization and integration into larger audio systems. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and installation needs in residential and commercial audio environments.</p></p>
