<p><ul><li>Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI)</li><li>Frozen Embryo Replacement (FER)</li><li>Pre-implantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IVF market encompasses various advanced reproductive technologies. Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) involves directly injecting a single sperm into an egg, enhancing fertilization chances in cases of male infertility. Frozen Embryo Replacement (FER) allows stored embryos to be thawed and implanted, offering flexibility in treatment timing. Pre-implantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD) enables genetic screening of embryos before implantation, ensuring the selection of healthy embryos and reducing the risk of genetic disorders. Together, these methods cater to diverse fertility needs and improve outcomes.</p></p>
