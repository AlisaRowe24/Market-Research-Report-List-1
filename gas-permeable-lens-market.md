<p><p>Gas permeable (GP) lenses are rigid contact lenses made from materials that allow oxygen to pass through to the cornea, promoting eye health while providing clear vision. These lenses are preferred for their durability, ease of care, and suitability for individuals with corneal irregularities, such as keratoconus. The market for gas permeable lenses is witnessing significant growth due to rising awareness about eye health, an increase in vision impairments, and advancements in lens technology.</p><p>The Gas Permeable Lens Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5% during the forecast period. Moreover, innovations in lens materials and design are enabling better oxygen transmission and comfort, appealing to a broader consumer base. </p><p>Latest trends indicate a growing preference for multifunctional GP lenses, such as those designed for digital device users, which help reduce eye strain.
