<p><p>The ink dispersants market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based dispersants are environmentally friendly, utilizing water as a solvent, making them suitable for use in various applications without harmful emissions. They offer good stability and adhesion. In contrast, solvent-based dispersants rely on organic solvents, providing excellent performance in terms of pigment distribution and durability, often preferred in industries requiring high-quality finishes. Both types cater to specific needs, balancing environmental concerns with performance requirements.</p></p>
