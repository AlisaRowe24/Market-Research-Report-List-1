<p><strong>The HVAC Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Valve</li><li>Globe Valve</li><li>Butterfly Valve</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVAC valve market comprises various types, including ball valves, globe valves, butterfly valves, and others. Ball valves provide quick shut-off and excellent flow control, ideal for on/off applications. Globe valves are used for throttling and regulating flow due to their design, allowing precise adjustments. Butterfly valves are lightweight and space-efficient, suitable for large-scale airflow management. Other valves, such as check and pressure relief valves, play crucial roles in system safety and performance, enhancing overall HVAC efficiency.</p></p>
