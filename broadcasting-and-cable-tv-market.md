<p><p>Broadcasting and cable TV markets encompass various transmission methods for delivering content. Terrestrial television relies on ground-based transmission towers to broadcast signals, accessible via antennas. Cable TV transmits signals through coaxial or fiber-optic cables, providing a wide range of channels and often bundled services like internet and phone. Satellite television uses satellites in orbit to deliver programming directly to satellite dishes, offering extensive channel options and coverage in remote areas. Each type caters to different viewer needs and technological capabilities.</p></p>
