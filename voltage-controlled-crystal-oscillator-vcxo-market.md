<p><p>A Voltage Controlled Crystal Oscillator (VCXO) is an electronic oscillator that uses a crystal as its frequency-determining element, with the frequency being adjustable by varying the input voltage. The VCXO market comprises different output types: PECL (Positive Emitter Coupled Logic) provides a high-speed output suitable for telecommunications; CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) offers low power consumption and is widely used in digital circuits; SINEWAVE provides a smooth, sinusoidal output ideal for analog applications. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing versatility in frequency modulation.</p></p>
