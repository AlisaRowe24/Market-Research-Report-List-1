<p><strong>The Dyestuff for Cotton Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Dyes</li><li>Disperse Dyes</li><li>Direct Dyes</li><li>Sulfur Dyes</li><li>Optical Brighteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cotton dyestuff market is segmented into several types based on application and chemical composition. Reactive dyes chemically bond with cotton fibers, providing vibrant and long-lasting colors. Disperse dyes are used primarily for synthetic fibers but can also color cotton blends. Direct dyes are applied directly to cotton without a mordant, offering bright hues but lower fastness. Sulfur dyes, known for their cost-effectiveness, produce rich shades but may lack vibrancy. Optical brighteners enhance the brightness and whiteness of cotton fabrics.</p></p>
