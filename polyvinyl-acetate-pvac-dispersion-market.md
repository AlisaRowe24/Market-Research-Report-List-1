<p><p>The Polyvinyl Acetate (PVAc) dispersion market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. </p><p>Water-based PVAc dispersions are eco-friendly, offering low volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them suitable for adhesives, paints, and coatings. </p><p>Solvent-based PVAc dispersions contain higher VOC content, providing strong bonding properties and durability, often used in industrial applications and formulations requiring quick drying times. Both types cater to diverse end-use industries, including construction, packaging, and textiles, reflecting varying regulatory and performance needs.</p></p>
