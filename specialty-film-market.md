<p><p>The specialty film market encompasses various types designed for specific applications. Stretch film is used for securing and bundling products, while shrink film contracts around items when heat is applied, providing a snug fit and protection. Barrier film protects goods from moisture and gases, enhancing shelf life. Conductive film is engineered to dissipate static electricity, making it ideal for electronic components. Other specialty films include biometric, biodegradable, and decorative films, catering to diverse industries and packaging requirements.</p></p>
