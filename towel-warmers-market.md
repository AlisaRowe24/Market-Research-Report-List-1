<p><strong>The Towel Warmers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The towel warmer market is primarily divided into two types: electric and hydronic. Electric towel warmers use electrical heating elements to warm towels and can be easily installed in any bathroom or space. They typically heat up quickly and offer adjustable temperature settings. Hydronic towel warmers, on the other hand, are connected to a home’s heating system, using hot water to warm the towels. They provide consistent warmth and are more energy-efficient, making them ideal for integrated heating solutions.</p></p>
