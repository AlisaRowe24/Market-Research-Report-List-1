<p><p>Invasive blood pressure monitoring sensors are categorized into three main types based on the number of channels: single channel, dual channel, and triple channel sensors. Single channel sensors measure blood pressure at one site, providing basic monitoring. Dual channel sensors enable simultaneous measurements from two sites, enhancing accuracy and patient data. Triple channel sensors take it a step further, offering real-time readings from three sites, thereby delivering comprehensive insight into a patient's hemodynamic status and facilitating advanced critical care management.</p></p>
