<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Type</li><li>Pneumatic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy-duty damper market comprises two primary types: hydraulic and pneumatic dampers. Hydraulic dampers utilize fluid pressure to absorb and dissipate energy, providing effective vibration control in industrial applications. They are known for their durability and precise control. Pneumatic dampers, on the other hand, use compressed air to manage airflow and reduce vibrations. These are often lighter and more responsive, making them suitable for environments requiring quick adjustments. Both types are essential for ensuring operational efficiency and equipment protection.</p></p>
