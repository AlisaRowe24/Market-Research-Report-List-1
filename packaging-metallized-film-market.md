<p><p>The packaging metallized film market includes various types such as PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) film, CPP (Cast Polypropylene) film, and BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) film. PET film is known for its excellent gas barrier properties and durability, making it ideal for food and beverage packaging. CPP film offers better clarity and sealability, commonly used for flexible packaging applications. BOPP film is lightweight and provides high tensile strength, frequently employed in snack and confectionery packaging. Each type enhances product preservation and shelf appeal.</p></p>
