<p><p>The semiconductor test probes market is categorized into several types based on material composition. Brass test probes offer good conductivity and are cost-effective but may corrode over time. Phosphor bronze test probes are known for their excellent elasticity and resistance to wear, making them suitable for high-volume testing. Nickel silver test probes balance conductivity and durability, while BeCu (Beryllium Copper) test probes provide high strength and thermal conductivity, ideal for demanding applications. Other probes may include specialized materials for unique testing needs.</p></p>
