<p><p>The rotary electrical joint market includes various types, such as mid-sized capsules, enclosed power thru-bore, and enclosed signal thru-bore joints. Mid-sized capsules are designed for applications requiring moderate power transmission and rotary movement. Enclosed power thru-bore joints provide robust power transfer in a sealed environment, minimizing wear and exposure. Enclosed signal thru-bore joints focus on maintaining high-quality signal transmission while protecting sensitive components from external factors, making them ideal for precision applications in rotating machinery.</p></p>
