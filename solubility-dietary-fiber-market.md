<p><p>The solubility dietary fiber market comprises various types, including cellulose, hemicellulose, chitin, and chitosan. Cellulose is a structural component of plant cell walls, providing bulk and aiding digestion. Hemicellulose, found alongside cellulose, enhances water retention and fermentation in the gut. Chitin, derived from crustacean exoskeletons, promotes gut health, while chitosan, a deacetylated form, is known for its potential weight management benefits. Other fibers include inulin and psyllium, which contribute to overall digestive wellness and health.</p></p>
