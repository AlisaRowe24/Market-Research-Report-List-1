<p><p>The Mobile Electrocardiograph Monitor market includes three primary types: Thoracometry, Legometry, and Hand Measurement. Thoracometry focuses on measuring heart activity through sensors placed on the chest, providing detailed cardiac data. Legometry uses specialized sensors on the leg to monitor heart rhythms, often beneficial for patients with mobility issues. Hand Measurement devices, typically portable and user-friendly, capture ECG signals through sensors on the hand. Each type caters to different patient needs and enhances cardiac monitoring flexibility and accessibility.</p></p>
