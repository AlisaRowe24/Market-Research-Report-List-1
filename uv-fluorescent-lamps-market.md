<p><ul><li>Instant Start Sterilization</li><li>Preheat Sterilization Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV fluorescent lamps market is categorized into two main types: instant start sterilization and preheat sterilization. Instant start sterilization lamps operate quickly, providing immediate ultraviolet light for disinfection purposes without a warm-up phase, making them ideal for applications requiring rapid sterilization. In contrast, preheat sterilization lamps require a brief warm-up period to reach optimal performance, offering consistent and efficient UV output for prolonged use. Both types are widely utilized in healthcare, water treatment, and air purification technologies.</p></p>
