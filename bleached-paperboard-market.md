<p><p>The bleached paperboard market comprises two primary types: single side bleached paperboard and double side bleached paperboard. Single side bleached paperboard features one side that is treated with a bleaching process, making it ideal for applications requiring a high-quality finish on one side, such as packaging and printing. In contrast, double side bleached paperboard undergoes bleaching on both sides, offering enhanced brightness and smoothness, making it suitable for high-end packaging and graphic applications where both sides are visible.</p></p>
