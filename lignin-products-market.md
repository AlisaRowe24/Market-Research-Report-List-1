<p><p>The lignin products market includes several key types: Kraft lignin, lignosulphonates, and other lignin derivatives. Kraft lignin is a high-purity byproduct of the kraft pulping process, used in applications like bioplastics and adhesives. Lignosulphonates are water-soluble byproducts from sulfite pulping, utilized in concrete additives and animal feed. Other lignin products encompass a range of compounds used in various industries, including pharmaceuticals and agriculture, highlighting lignin's versatility and potential for sustainable material applications.</p></p>
