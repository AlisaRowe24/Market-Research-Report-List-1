<p><strong>The Front and Rear Air-Conditioning (AC) Thermal Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop System</li><li>Closed Loop System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front and rear air-conditioning (AC) thermal systems regulate cabin temperature in vehicles. Open-loop systems use external air for cooling, expelling heat outside, making them simpler but less efficient in extreme conditions. Closed-loop systems recirculate internal air, maintaining consistent temperatures and improving energy efficiency, especially in harsh climates. Both systems cater to diverse market needs, with open-loop systems being cost-effective for basic applications, while closed-loop systems are preferred for advanced, energy-efficient solutions in premium vehicles or extreme environments.</p></p>
