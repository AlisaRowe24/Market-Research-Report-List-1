<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer Composites (CFRPs) are advanced materials combining carbon fibers with polymer matrices, enhancing strength and reducing weight. The market includes metal matrix composites (MMCs) and ceramic matrix composites (CMCs). MMCs combine carbon fibers with metals, offering superior thermal conductivity and strength, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. CMCs, on the other hand, integrate carbon fibers within ceramic materials, providing excellent high-temperature resistance and wear characteristics, suitable for applications in defense and energy sectors.</p></p>
