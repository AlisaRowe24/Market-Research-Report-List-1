<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC)** maintains safe following distances by automatically adjusting vehicle speed.</p><p>2. **Blind Spot Detection (BSD)** alerts drivers of vehicles in adjacent lanes, enhancing safety during lane changes.</p><p>3. **Forward Collision Warning (FCW)** monitors traffic ahead and warns drivers of impending collisions.</p><p>4. **Lane Departure Warning System (LDWS)** alerts drivers when they unintentionally drift out of their lane.</p><p>5. **Park Assist System (PAS)** assists with parking maneuvers. </p><p>Other systems encompass various emerging technologies aimed at improving driving safety and efficiency.</p></p>
