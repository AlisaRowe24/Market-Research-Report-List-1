<p><strong>The Cable Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IntumescentCableCoatings</li><li>Non-IntumescentCableCoatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cable coating market is primarily divided into two types: intumescent and non-intumescent coatings. Intumescent cable coatings expand and char when exposed to high temperatures, forming an insulating barrier that protects cables from fire damage. In contrast, non-intumescent coatings provide a direct protective layer against environmental factors, chemicals, and mechanical damage but do not expand under heat. Both types are crucial for enhancing the fire safety and durability of electrical cables in various applications.</p></p>
