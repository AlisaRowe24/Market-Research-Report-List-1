<p><p>The military aerospace landing gear market is primarily categorized into two types: main landing gear and nose/tail landing gear. Main landing gear typically supports the majority of the aircraft's weight during landing and takeoff, providing stability and structural integrity. In contrast, nose landing gear (or tail landing gear) is crucial for steering and directional control during taxiing and landing operations. Both types are essential for ensuring safe landings, durability, and overall aircraft performance in military applications.</p></p>
