<p><p>Shopping cart software can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based shopping cart software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access their e-commerce platforms via the internet, facilitating easy scalability and maintenance. Conversely, on-premise shopping cart software is installed directly on a user’s local servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring more resources for maintenance and updates. Both types cater to different business needs regarding infrastructure and operational flexibility.</p></p>
