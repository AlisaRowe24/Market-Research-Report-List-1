<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tyre</li><li>Bias Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle tire market primarily includes radial and bias tires. Radial tires feature a construction where the cords run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, longer tread life, and improved handling, making them ideal for long-haul and heavy-duty applications. Bias tires have crisscrossed cord layers, providing robust sidewalls and higher load capacity, suitable for off-road or rugged terrains. Radial tires dominate modern markets due to performance advantages, while bias tires remain relevant for specific industrial or agricultural uses.</p></p>
