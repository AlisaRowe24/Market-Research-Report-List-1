<p><strong>The Textile Dyestuff Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disperse Dyes</li><li>Reactive Dyes</li><li>Sulfur Dyes</li><li>Vat Dyes</li><li>Acid Dyes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textile dyestuff market comprises various types, each tailored for specific applications. Disperse dyes are primarily used for synthetic fibers like polyester, providing vibrant colors and high fastness. Reactive dyes bond chemically with fibers, ideal for cotton and other cellulosics. Sulfur dyes offer excellent wash and light fastness for dark shades, commonly used in denim. Vat dyes provide deep, long-lasting colors, particularly for cotton and wool. Acid dyes are suitable for protein fibers and nylon, known for their bright hues and ease of use.</p></p>
