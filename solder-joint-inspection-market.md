<p><strong>The Solder Joint Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI)</li><li>Automatic X-Ray Inspection (AXI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solder joint inspection market encompasses various technologies for ensuring the quality and reliability of solder joints in electronics manufacturing. Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) utilizes high-resolution cameras to visually inspect solder joints, identifying defects such as insufficient solder or misalignment. In contrast, Automatic X-Ray Inspection (AXI) employs X-ray technology to examine hidden solder joints, especially in multi-layer PCBs, detecting issues like voids and insufficient solder beneath the surface. Both methods enhance manufacturing accuracy and reduce the risk of failures.</p></p>
