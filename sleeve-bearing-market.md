<p><strong>The Sleeve Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Sleeve Bearing</li><li>Axial Sleeve Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sleeve bearing market is segmented into two primary types: radial sleeve bearings and axial sleeve bearings. Radial sleeve bearings support loads perpendicular to the shaft, providing smooth rotation and reduced friction, making them ideal for applications like electric motors and pumps. In contrast, axial sleeve bearings handle thrust loads parallel to the shaft, suitable for machinery requiring resistance to axial forces, such as in gearboxes and turbines. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing machinery efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
