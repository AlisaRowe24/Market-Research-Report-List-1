<p><p>The chronic drainage catheters market encompasses various types, primarily including chest drainage catheters and external ventricular drainage catheters. Chest drainage catheters are designed to remove air, fluid, or pus from the pleural space, aiding respiratory functions. External ventricular drainage catheters are used to manage cerebrospinal fluid in cases of hydrocephalus or brain injury, preventing increased intracranial pressure. Other types may include catheter systems for different anatomical locations or specific conditions, catering to diverse medical needs in drainage management.</p></p>
