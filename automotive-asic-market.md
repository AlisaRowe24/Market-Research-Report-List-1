<p><strong>The Automotive ASIC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Cell Based ASIC</li><li>Gate Array Based ASIC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive ASIC market includes Standard Cell Based ASICs and Gate Array Based ASICs. Standard Cell Based ASICs are custom-designed using pre-defined logic cells, offering high performance, flexibility, and optimized power consumption, ideal for advanced automotive applications like ADAS and infotainment. Gate Array Based ASICs use pre-fabricated silicon wafers with customizable interconnects, providing faster development cycles and lower costs, suitable for simpler automotive functions. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient, reliable, and application-specific solutions in the automotive industry.</p></p>
