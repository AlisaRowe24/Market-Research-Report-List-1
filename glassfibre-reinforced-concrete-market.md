<p><p>The Glassfibre Reinforced Concrete (GRC) market features three primary types: the Spray Process, Premix Process, and Hybrid Process. The Spray Process involves applying a glassfiber-reinforced mixture onto surfaces using spray technology, ideal for complex shapes. The Premix Process combines glass fibers with the concrete mix before forming, ensuring uniform strength throughout. The Hybrid Process merges both techniques, offering flexibility and enhanced performance for varied applications. Together, these methods cater to diverse construction needs, optimizing durability and aesthetics.</p></p>
