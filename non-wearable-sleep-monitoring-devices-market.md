<p><strong>The Non-wearable Sleep Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-wearable sleep monitoring devices are categorized into contact and contactless types. Contact devices typically involve sensors placed under the mattress or bedding, measuring variables such as heart rate and movement to analyze sleep patterns. In contrast, contactless devices utilize advanced technologies like radar or infrared sensors to monitor sleep without physical contact, assessing movements, breathing rates, and sleep stages. Both types aim to enhance sleep quality and provide insights for better sleep management without the need for wearable technology.</p></p>
