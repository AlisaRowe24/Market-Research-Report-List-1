<p><strong>The Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Method</li><li>Dry Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen (CRMB) is produced using two primary methods: wet and dry. In the wet method, crumb rubber is integrated into the bitumen during the production process, resulting in a uniform mix that enhances the bitumen's properties. Conversely, the dry method involves adding crumb rubber to the aggregate before mixing with bitumen, allowing for a more flexible pavement structure. Both methods improve durability and performance in road applications, catering to different performance needs and preferences in the asphalt market.</p></p>
