<p><p>The Synthetic Motor Oils market comprises two main types: Semi-Synthetic and Full Synthetic Motor Oils. Semi-Synthetic Motor Oil blends conventional and synthetic base oils, offering improved performance, protection, and fuel efficiency over traditional oils at a moderate cost. Full Synthetic Motor Oil is entirely engineered from chemically modified compounds, providing superior lubrication, thermal stability, and engine protection, especially under extreme conditions.
