<p><ul><li>Horizontal Axis Wind Turbine</li><li>Vertical Axis Wind Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small wind power market primarily consists of Horizontal Axis Wind Turbines (HAWT) and Vertical Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT). HAWTs are the most common type, featuring blades mounted horizontally, which are more efficient for capturing wind energy, making them suitable for both residential and commercial applications. In contrast, VAWTs have blades that are vertically oriented, allowing them to harness wind from any direction, making them ideal for urban settings or turbulent wind conditions. Both types cater to a growing demand for renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
