<p><p>The PE blow molded containers market is primarily categorized into two types: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene). HDPE containers are known for their strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals, making them ideal for packaging a variety of products, including food and industrial goods. In contrast, LDPE containers are more flexible and thinner, often used for less demanding applications like squeeze bottles and flexible pouches. Both types serve distinct needs across industries, ensuring functionality and safety in packaging solutions.</p></p>
