<p><p>Site-to-Site VPNs are categorized into two primary market types: intranet-based and extranet-based. Intranet-based VPNs connect multiple branches of the same organization, facilitating secure communication and data sharing within the corporate network. In contrast, extranet-based VPNs link different organizations, allowing secure information exchange between partners, suppliers, or clients while keeping sensitive data protected. Both types enhance collaboration and security but serve distinct purposes based on the nature of the relationships involved.</p></p>
