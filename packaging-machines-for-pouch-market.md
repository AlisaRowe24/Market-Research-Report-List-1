<p><p>The pouch packaging machines market includes several types, primarily Horizontal Form Fill Seal (HFFS) machines and Vertical Form Fill Seal (VFFS) machines. HFFS machines create pouches horizontally, suitable for various products, providing high-speed packaging. In contrast, VFFS machines produce pouches vertically, ideal for granules, powders, and liquids, ensuring efficient filling.
