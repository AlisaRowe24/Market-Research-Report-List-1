<p><p>The UV LED sterilization and disinfection market is categorized into three main types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C LEDs. UV-A LEDs, with wavelengths of 320-400 nm, are primarily used for curing applications but have limited disinfection capabilities. UV-B LEDs (280-320 nm) are effective for certain biological applications, promoting Vitamin D synthesis but not widely used for sterilization. UV-C LEDs, emitting wavelengths of 100-280 nm, are the most effective for killing microorganisms, making them crucial for sterilization and disinfection applications in various industries.</p></p>
