<p><p>Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) Systems are technologies designed for converting thermal energy into electrical energy using organic fluids with low boiling points. ORC systems are gaining traction due to their ability to enhance energy efficiency and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.</p><p>The market for ORC systems is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for sustainable energy solutions and advancements in technology.
