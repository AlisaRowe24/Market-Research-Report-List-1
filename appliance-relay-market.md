<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Relay</li><li>Solid State Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The appliance relay market is primarily divided into two types: electromagnetic relays and solid-state relays. Electromagnetic relays utilize an electromagnetic coil to mechanically switch contacts, offering high durability and reliability, suitable for various applications. In contrast, solid-state relays use semiconductor devices to switch, providing faster response times, greater efficiency, and improved longevity. Both types cater to different needs in consumer electronics and industrial applications, offering solutions for controlling electrical loads in various environments.</p></p>
