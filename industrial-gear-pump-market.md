<p><strong>The Industrial Gear Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Gear Pumps</li><li>External Gear Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial gear pump market is primarily divided into two types: internal gear pumps and external gear pumps. Internal gear pumps consist of a rotor and a gear, with fluid being transported between the inner gear and an outer casing, providing high efficiency and low pulsation. In contrast, external gear pumps utilize two interlocking gears, creating a precise volume of fluid transfer, suitable for higher flow rates. Both types are widely used across industries for their reliability and ability to handle various viscosities.</p></p>
